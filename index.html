SME Workplace Health Package

The SME+ Workplace Health Package is an initiative led by the Health Promotion Board

with MMG as the appointed Programme Manager.

Through a panel of qualified service providers, SMEs can now tap on a gamut of health

activities spanning Active Living, Healthy Living Chronic Disease and Mental Wellness.

Programmes receive Co-Funding from the Health Promotion Board according to the following guidelines.

programme funding

Programme Categories Co-Funding Description
Chronic Disease Management 80%

To provide accessible platforms for employees to participate in health screening.

To increase understanding of chronic diseases.
Mental Welbeing 40%

To impart knowledge for having good mental wellbeing, which includes emotional and social

intelligence, resilience and cognitive efficacy.Tq apply these knowledge at work and home.

Active Living 50%

To improve understanding and lower perceived barriers to physical activities.

To inculcate good exercise habits.
Healthy Eating 50%

To increase understanding of individual caloric requirements and ways to balance

daily calories input and outpt.To lower perceived barriers to healthier food options.

(Barriers include cost,accessibility to healthier food and taste)
Capacity Building 40%

To increase knowledge and skiils required to provide support to fellow colleagues
